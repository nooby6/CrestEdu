Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What age groups do you serve?

We provide educational services for students from kindergarten through high school (ages 5-18), as well as adult education programs. Our curriculum is tailored to each age group's developmental needs.

How do I know which program is right for my child?

We offer free consultations to assess your child's learning style, academic needs, and goals. Our educational specialists will recommend the best program based on this evaluation.

Are your programs accredited?

Yes, all our homeschooling programs are fully accredited and meet state education standards. Credits earned through our programs are transferable to other educational institutions.

What technology requirements are needed for online learning?

You'll need a computer or tablet with internet access, a webcam, and a microphone. We provide technical support to help you set up and troubleshoot any issues.
